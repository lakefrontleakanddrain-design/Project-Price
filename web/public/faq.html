Home Improvement Cost FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about home renovation and repair costs in 2026.

How much does a kitchen remodel cost?
A kitchen remodel costs $10,000–$75,000+ in 2026. Minor updates (paint, hardware, lighting) run $5,000–$15,000. A mid-range remodel with new cabinets and countertops averages $25,000–$45,000. A full gut remodel runs $50,000–$100,000+. Costs vary significantly by region. Full kitchen remodel cost guide →
How much does a bathroom remodel cost?
Bathroom remodels range from $5,000 for a basic refresh to $30,000+ for a full primary bath renovation. A mid-range full bathroom averages $10,000–$18,000. Full bathroom remodel cost guide →
How much does roof replacement cost?
$5,500–$12,000 for asphalt shingles on an average US home. Metal roofing runs $12,000–$25,000. Premium materials like slate or tile can exceed $35,000. Full roof replacement cost guide →
How much does HVAC replacement cost?
A full HVAC system (furnace + AC) costs $7,000–$15,000 installed. Central AC only runs $4,000–$8,000. Heat pumps range $8,000–$18,000. Full HVAC cost guide →
How much does flooring installation cost?
$3–$25+ per square foot installed. Carpet runs $2–$7/sq ft. LVP costs $4–$11/sq ft. Solid hardwood runs $10–$20+/sq ft. Full flooring cost guide →
How much does window replacement cost?
$300–$1,200 per window installed. Whole-home replacement of 10–15 windows runs $5,000–$15,000. Full window replacement cost guide →
How much does a deck cost?
$8,000–$35,000 depending on size and material. A 200 sq ft pressure-treated deck averages $8,000–$12,000. Composite decks run $20,000–$35,000 for 400 sq ft. Full deck cost guide →
How much does fence installation cost?
$15–$60+ per linear foot installed. A 150-linear-foot backyard fence costs $3,000–$8,000. Wood privacy fence averages $20–$45/ft; vinyl runs $25–$55/ft. Full fence cost guide →
How much does water heater replacement cost?
$800–$1,800 for a standard tank model installed. Tankless models run $1,500–$3,500. Heat pump water heaters cost $1,200–$2,800. Full water heater cost guide →
How much does a new garage cost?
Building a new 2-car garage (replacing a 1-car) costs $35,000–$90,000+. Detached garages run $25,000–$60,000 depending on size and finish. Full garage cost guide →
How much does basement structural repair cost?
$5,000–$80,000+ depending on severity. Carbon fiber strap reinforcement runs $5,000–$15,000. Full wall replacement runs $20,000–$80,000+. Full basement structural cost guide →
