Frequently Asked Questions

Is it worth replacing old windows?

If your windows are single-pane, 20+ years old, or have broken seals (fogging between panes), replacement typically pays off through energy savings, comfort improvement, and added home value. ENERGY STAR certified windows can reduce energy bills by 15–25%.

How long does window replacement take?

An experienced installer replaces 2–4 windows per day. A whole-home project with 12 windows usually completes in 1–2 days.

Can I replace windows myself?

Insert/retrofit replacements (fitting new window into existing frame) are DIY-friendly. Full-frame replacements that involve removing exterior trim and potentially siding are best left to professionals, as improper installation can cause water infiltration and structural damage.
