Data Deletion Request

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data from ProjectPrice at any time. Complete the form below or email us directly and we will process your request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

When you submit a deletion request, we will remove:

  • Your phone number and authentication record
  • Your estimate request history and project photos
  • Any session tokens associated with your account
  • Any contractor account data if you are registered as a contractor

Note: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you have active estimate requests in progress, those will also be cancelled. You can always create a new account in the future.

Submit Your Request

Or Contact Us Directly

You can also submit a privacy-related message through our secure contact form. Include the subject line "Data Deletion Request" in your message.

  • Support form: ProjectPrice Contact Page

We will confirm receipt of your request within 5 business days and complete deletion within 30 days.
