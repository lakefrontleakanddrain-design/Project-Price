Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

ProjectPrice ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at project-price-app.netlify.app and the ProjectPrice mobile application (together, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Mobile App — Phone Number

When you use the ProjectPrice mobile app, we ask for your mobile phone number to verify your identity via a one-time SMS passcode (OTP). We use this phone number solely to:

  • Send you a verification SMS via Twilio
  • Identify you for estimate requests and project history
  • Route your estimate request to a matched contractor

We do not store your phone number on our own servers beyond the duration of your authenticated session. Your phone number is not sold, shared for marketing, or used for promotional SMS.

Project Photos & Descriptions

When you submit an estimate request, you optionally provide photos and a text description of your project. This information is used solely to generate your AI cost estimate and, if you request an exact quote, to share with the matched contractor so they can prepare for your project.

Contractor Account Information

Contractors who register with ProjectPrice provide a name, email address, phone number, trade type, and service area. This information is used to match contractors with relevant homeowner estimate requests and to manage their account.

Website — Analytics & Usage Data

When you visit our website we automatically collect standard usage information through third-party analytics tools, including pages visited, browser type, device type, and general geographic region. This data is aggregated and anonymous and is used only to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity and authenticate your session
  • To generate AI-powered cost estimates for your project
  • To route your exact quote request to a matched contractor
  • To communicate with you about your estimate or account
  • To improve the accuracy and quality of our AI estimator

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Twilio — SMS delivery for OTP verification. Twilio Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — Authentication and database. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Netlify — Website and serverless function hosting. Netlify Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI — AI-powered cost estimation. OpenAI Privacy Policy

4. SMS Messaging

ProjectPrice sends transactional SMS messages to users who have explicitly opted in via a consent checkbox on our website forms. These messages relate to project requests, estimate updates, contractor matching, account activity, and access to the My Estimates dashboard. SMS opt-in is optional and is not required to use our services.

Msg & data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help or contact support@projectprice.app.

Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. This information is collected solely to comply with messaging regulations and to authenticate the consent record.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. When you request an exact quote, your project details (photos, description, and contact information) are shared exclusively with the single matched contractor for that request. No other parties receive your information.

6. Data Retention

Session tokens expire automatically after your session ends. Project estimate data is retained to allow you to review your history within the app. You may request deletion of your data at any time — see Section 8.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures including HTTPS encryption and secure authentication tokens. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To request data deletion, visit our Data Deletion page or contact us directly.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
