About ProjectPrice™

ProjectPrice gives homeowners instant, realistic cost estimates for any home improvement project — powered by AI and calibrated to real contractor pricing in your local US market. Free to use. No account required to get an estimate.

What We Do

ProjectPrice bridges the information gap between homeowners and the contractor market. Before you call a single contractor, before you commit to a scope of work, ProjectPrice lets you understand what your project should cost — based on real regional pricing data for your specific area of the country.

Upload a photo of your project space. Describe what you want done. Get three instant cost tiers — Basic, Standard, and Premium — showing the realistic range of what licensed contractors in your market will charge.

Who Uses ProjectPrice

Homeowners planning renovations — Know your budget before you start calling contractors. Avoid sticker shock and enter contractor conversations informed.

Home buyers and sellers — Quickly estimate repair costs during a transaction to negotiate repair credits or price adjustments accurately.

Real estate agents — Give clients instant repair cost context during showings and negotiations without guessing.

Property managers — Get rapid budget estimates for maintenance and improvement projects across your portfolio.

Our Coverage

ProjectPrice covers all 50 US states with regional pricing data calibrated to local labor markets. From rural Midwest markets to coastal metros, our estimates reflect what contractors in your area actually charge — not a national average that may be off by 30–50% for your location.

Covered project types include: kitchen remodels, bathroom renovations, roofing, HVAC, flooring, painting, windows, doors, siding, decks, fences, garages, basements, structural repairs, plumbing, electrical, and more.

Our Commitment

ProjectPrice provides pricing guidance based solely on customer-submitted information. We are not a contractor and do not perform work. Estimates are planning tools — not binding quotes. All final pricing is confirmed by the selected licensed contractor after an onsite visit.
